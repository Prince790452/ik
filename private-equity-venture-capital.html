Private Equity (PE) and Venture Capital (VC) are powerful tools for financing innovation, scaling enterprises, and driving economic transformation. In Uganda, the legal and regulatory landscape is evolving to accommodate these investment models, with recent reforms offering clarity and investor confidence.

üìò Legal Foundations

  • Capital Markets Authority Act: Empowers CMA to regulate PE/VC funds and issue licensing frameworks.

  • Partnerships Act & LLP Regulations, 2025: Operationalizes Limited Liability Partnerships as fund vehicles.
  • Investment Code Act, 2019: Provides incentives and registration for qualifying investors.

  • Monitoring: Board participation, performance metrics, ESG compliance.
  • Exit: IPO, trade sale, secondary buyout, or management buyback.
