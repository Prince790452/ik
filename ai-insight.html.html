5. Uganda‚Äôs Legal and Regulatory Landscape

  • Data Protection and Privacy Act (2019): Regulates personal data used in AI systems
  • Computer Misuse Act (2011): Criminalizes unauthorized access to digital systems
  • Electronic Transactions Act (2011): Supports digital contracts and transactions
  • Copyright and Industrial Property Laws: Raise complex questions about AI-generated IP

6. Policy Developments and Challenges

  • National AI Strategy: Under development by the Uganda National Council for Science and Technology (UNCST)
  • Vision 2040: Identifies AI as a key driver of socioeconomic transformation
  • Calls for reform: Parliamentarians urge the drafting of AI-specific legislation

Uganda must harmonize tech innovation with inclusive and rights-based governance.

7. Legal Gaps and Recommendations

  • Establish an AI Act that governs transparency, fairness, and accountability
  • Recognize AI-generated works in intellectual property law
  • Empower the Personal Data Protection Office to oversee algorithmic auditing
  • Create a national ethics council for AI oversight
  • Introduce regulatory sandboxes for safe experimentation
